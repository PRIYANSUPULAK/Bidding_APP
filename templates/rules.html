Few Rules which Everyone Has to follow while bidding.

1. You must me logged in before making any bid or posting a product for sell.


2. A product remains on the auction page for a maximum of 24 hours from the time of being posted.


3. UTC time(Not your local time) is displayed on the page.


4. User with the highest bidding at the endtime of a product will be declared as the winner.


5. Bidding will be done in the following way:

For product price less than RS.1000::: Bidding will increase with the step of Rs.10

For product price less than RS.10000 and more then RS.1000::: Bidding will increase with the step of Rs.100

For product price greater than RS.10000::: Bidding will increase with the step of Rs.1000
